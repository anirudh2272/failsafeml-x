# Patent-Screening Memo

## Scope

This memo is a preliminary technical note, not legal advice.

## Potentially Interesting Direction

The potentially protectable direction is not general uncertainty estimation, conformal prediction, drift detection, human review, or routing individually. Those areas have substantial prior art.

The more specific direction worth later review is the integrated decision envelope that combines calibrated uncertainty, drift/OOD risk, explicit failure taxonomy, repair-plan generation, and a cost-sensitive routing policy for model-agnostic ML reliability operations.

## Current Position

Patentability is not claimed. A real prior-art search would be required before any patent claim.

## Risk

Most individual components are known. The novelty, if any, would likely depend on a specific combination, workflow, scoring formulation, or operational integration.
